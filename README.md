# 👋 About

I am **Dr. Siva Ram Krishna Vadali**, **Scientist–G** at **CSIR–Central Mechanical Engineering Research Institute (CSIR-CMERI)**, Ministry of Science & Technology, Government of India, and **Adjunct Faculty** at the **Academy of Scientific and Innovative Research (AcSIR)**.

My research spans **Mathematical Statistics, Statistical Signal Processing, Estimation & Detection Theory, Communication Systems, Intelligent Sensing, Artificial Intelligence and Autonomous Engineering Systems**. Over the past two decades, my work has evolved from mathematical theory to the realization of deployable engineering systems addressing strategic, industrial and marine applications.

# 💡 Research Philosophy

My research is driven by the belief that the ultimate value of mathematics lies in solving practical engineering problems. While application domains have expanded from communication systems and remote sensing to intelligent sensing and autonomous underwater robotics, the underlying objective has remained unchanged: **Signal Processing for Information Extraction**.

Rather than pursuing mathematical optimality alone, my work emphasizes inference procedures that are analytically rigorous, computationally efficient and practically deployable. This philosophy continues to guide my research in statistical signal processing, intelligent sensing and autonomous engineering systems.

> **Research Vision**
>
> *Mathematics provides the foundation. Signal processing extracts information. Intelligent sensing bridges the physical world with computation. Engineering transforms these principles into technologies that address real-world challenges.*
